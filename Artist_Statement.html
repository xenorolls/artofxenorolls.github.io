Why Create?

I make art simply for the joy of it. Whether the project pertains to hand-crafted sculpture or digital 3D models, I create purely for my sake of creation. Sure, the work might lean to themes or expression, or to take inspiration from cultures to media, but my work ultimately serves as a channel to release my ideas into given form. Whether my goal is to make for an animation or to have a model ready for 3D printing, my motivation for pursuing art is the enjoyment in making them.
